FAQ

Question
Do I need admissions Support Counselor?

It's different for everyone! If you're not an expert in college admissions, eager to make sure everything is set up for a positive outcome, and want to reduce the stress of the process, then working with a counselor to navigate the college planning path could be a great option for you. An exceptional college counselor will familiarize themselves with your objectives and provide comprehensive guidance to you, throughout the entire admissions process, including:

  • Developing your applicant profile
  • Helping you to choose where to apply
  • Editing and refining your essays
  • Polishing your applications
  • Additionally! 
College counseling might not be the perfect fit for everyone, with numerous individuals achieving success in college applications through the use of free resources and guidance from their school counselor. Nonetheless, if you're seeking a more seamless and fruitful experience, considering our college counseling services could be a beneficial choice for you.
Question
What is the cost of college counseling services?

The expense associated with college counseling varies based on the assistance you need. At Bright Futures Hub, we customize our services to meet the specific requirements of each family. This is the reason we provide a complimentary consultation to those considering our services. In this consultation, we dedicate time to understanding your family's unique situation, allowing us to suggest a program that aligns with your objectives and financial plan.

Question
What is the ideal time to begin college counseling?

At Bright Futures Hub, we support students from the 8th grade (Senior 2) all the way through their senior year. Beginning this journey earlier allows us to more effectively assist them in choosing the appropriate high school courses, engaging in extracurricular activities, securing internships, developing skills for success, and much more. An early start also facilitates a smoother, more relaxed approach to navigating these processes.
